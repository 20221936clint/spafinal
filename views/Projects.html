Bouncing Ball

The Bouncing Ball Animation project is an engaging and interactive application that simulates the physics of a bouncing ball. This project is designed to provide both visual appeal and educational value,
allowing users to explore the principles of motion, gravity, and elasticity in a fun and interactive way..

Hover to internet

The "hover to internet" interaction refers to the practice of using the hover functionality to enhance user experience on websites
or internet applications. When users move their cursor over certain elements, such as buttons, links, or images, the interface responds dynamically to provide feedback or reveal additional information.
